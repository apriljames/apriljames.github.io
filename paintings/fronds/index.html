Fronds : Watercolor on Paper : 2017

I keep thinking that I'm being cool and original when I draw a little egg on the shirt. Then I'll step back and look at the rest of my sketches and realize that it has become a heavily used signature item. I need to branch out on my shirt designs... Also, for a good two weeks or so after painting this, my pinterest explore page consisted of nothing but variations of ferns after looking at so many reference pictures. Most of them were definitely not necessary to search up, but plants are just too addicting to look at.
